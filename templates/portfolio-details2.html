Business Solution for Local Law Firm

Designed for a Local Law Firm this script saves hours of work a day by automatically searching for judicial processes updates and notifying the lawyers when that happens.

The script is able to read Judicial Processes numbers provided by the lawyers on an online Google Spreadsheet, open the Tribunal website, search for the corresponding process and check for the latest updates on the case. After that the script will format the data and dates to check if any of latest update is newer than the date it had on the previous scan. After finding new updates, the script will gather the data and send it via email to the lawyer responsible.
This script ends up saving at least 2 hours a day as the lawyer responsible doesn't have to manually search each judicial process on the website to check what is new.
